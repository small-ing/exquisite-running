What is it?

StrideSense is an AI model trained on professional runners that utilizes computer vision to process your videos and provide a holistic view of your technique so that you can run your fastest.

Read More

How does it work?

We take a video of a running person and return quantitative data based on their skeleton frame-by-frame. This data is processed by our AI model and interpreted into real-life metrics, which include their body angles, the time to stride, foot impact, etc., to optimize running form.

How to Use StrideSense

Set Up

Find an open well lit area, prop up your phone, and hit record.

Record

When recording, run in a straight line perpendicular to the camera for the best results.

Upload

Now that you've recorded yourself running, it's time for StrideSense to do the rest! Upload the video to our model and get results in seconds.
